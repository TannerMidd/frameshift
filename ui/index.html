          ENGINEERING PLANNER pin a blueprint · see what's missing · trade the gap at a material trader
          New to engineering? Read this first

          Engineers are characters who upgrade your ship's modules far beyond anything you can buy — for example, making your jump drive reach ~50% farther. Upgrades don't cost credits: they cost materials you collect while playing (scan data, salvage, planet minerals). Each upgrade climbs grades 1→5, applied several times per grade.

          Pin a blueprint below and this planner tracks every material the whole upgrade needs, ticks them off as you collect them, and tells you when a material trader (a station service that swaps materials: 6 lower-grade → 1 higher, 1 higher → 3 lower) can cover a shortfall from things you already have.

          Counts cover the whole grade 1→target climb using typical roll counts — treat them as a close estimate, not an exact bill.
